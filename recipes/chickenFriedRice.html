Chicken Fried Rice

Description

Better than takeout Chicken Fried Rice

Ingrediants

  • rice
  • carrots
  • chicken
  • eggs
  • peas
  • garlic
  • green onions
  • yellow onions
  • black pepper
  • salt
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil

Instructions

  1. cook rice in sauce pan
  2. peal and mince garlic, onion and carrots
  3. heat skillet
  4. cut and season chicken
  5. add oil to skillet and cook chicken
  6. add diced Ingrediantsto skillet
  7. whisk eggs
  8. mix eggs and vegtables
  9. fluff rice with fork
  10. add rice, chicken and soy sauce to vegtables
  11. sprinkle with green onions
  12. serve in bowl
