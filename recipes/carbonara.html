Carbonara

Description

Delicious Garlicky spaghetti with Parmasan Carbonara

Ingrediants

  • bacon
  • eggs
  • garlick
  • cream
  • parsley
  • spaghetti pasta
  • parmesan cheese
  • black pepper
  • salt

Instructions

  1. boil water in pot and add pasta
  2. preheat skillet
  3. chop and cook bacon
  4. combine cream and eggs
  5. add parmesan to mixture
  6. when pasta is done, drain water
  7. press garlick
  8. return pasta to pot and add bacin fat
  9. if sauce seems dry add reserved pasta water
  10. add parsley to pot
  11. dish and serve
