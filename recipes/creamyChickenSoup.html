Creamy Chicken Soup

Description

Creamy chicken and mushroom soup with rice

Ingrediants

  • rice
  • carrots
  • celery
  • chicken breasts
  • chicken or vegstable broth
  • ceremini mushrooms
  • thyme
  • garlic
  • milk
  • yellow onion
  • flour
  • black pepper
  • butter
  • salt

Instructions

  1. Wash and dry produce
  2. dice garlic and onions
  3. slice mushrooms
  4. slice carrots and dice celery
  5. preheat large pot
  6. add butter and coat bottom
  7. Add veggies to pot
  8. dice and season chicken
  9. slide thyme leaves off stems
  10. add chicken and thyme to pot
  11. add flour
  12. add broth milk and rice
  13. stir until simmer
  14. serve in bowls
